The Balance Method

What sets us apart

We believe Pilates works best in community. These four pillars guide everything we do — from class design to instructor training to how we welcome new members.

🤝
Community First

We keep classes small (max 12) so instructors know every member by name and every body by history.

📈
Progressive Method

A structured path from Fundamentals to Advanced. We track your progression so you always know what's next.

🧠
Mind-Body Connection

Every cue connects the breath to the movement. Pilates is as much a mental practice as a physical one.

🏥
Injury-Informed

All instructors are trained in common modifications. We work with your physio and your body, not against them.

Class Formats

Find your class

From your very first reformer session to advanced cardio sequences — we have a format designed for every stage of your practice.

All Levels50 min
Classic Reformer

The signature Balance class. Small group (12 max) on the reformer. Foundational exercises with instructor hands-on guidance throughout.

Intermediate55 min
Reformer Flow

Fluid, continuous sequences building heat and coordination. Ideal for members who have completed at least 5 Classic classes.

Intermediate–Advanced45 min
Cardio Reformer

Jump board, high-rep spring work, and elevated heart rate throughout. Pilates meets HIIT — all on the reformer.

All Levels45 min
Reformer Stretch

Slow, restorative reformer work focused on lengthening, mobility, and deep muscle release. Perfect as a standalone or recovery session.

Beginner50 min
Mat Fundamentals

Classical Pilates mat work in a supported group environment. Excellent for new clients, post-rehab, or anyone returning after a break.

1-on-1 or duet instruction. Fully tailored to your body, goals, and injuries. Any apparatus available.
